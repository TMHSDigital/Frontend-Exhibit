Rewind OS Catalogue
Read Me

This machine is a museum piece that still runs. Every window you are looking at exists in five parallel decades at once: the phosphor terminal of 1977, the one-bit desktop of 1984, the beveled grey of 1992, the wet gloss of 2004, and the flat quiet of 2013.

Drag the timeline below (or press the arrow keys) and the whole operating system re-dresses itself. Nothing else changes. The paint stays painted, the song keeps its notes, the clock keeps its time. Only the chrome travels.

Windows drag by their title bars. Double-click a title bar to roll the window up like a shade. Icons on the left reopen anything you close.
